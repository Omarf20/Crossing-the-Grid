# Crossing The Grid

A two-player board strategy game created as a final group project for **MTST195**.

**Game concept by Sam.**  
**Created by Sam, Mia, Omar and Jovin.**

---

## How to play

**Objective:** Move all 5 of your pieces to the opposite side of the board. First player to clear all pieces wins.

**Player 1 (blue X)** starts on rows 1–2 and moves toward row 5.  
**Player 2 (orange O)** starts on rows 4–5 and moves toward row 1.

### Legal moves
- Move 1 step horizontally or vertically to an empty square.
- Jump over **your own piece** horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — if the square directly on the other side is empty.
- You cannot jump over or land on your opponent's piece.
- A piece is scored and removed the moment it reaches the opposite side row.

### Optional rule
- **No backward moves** — pieces cannot move toward their own starting side (can be toggled in-game).

---

## Features
- Two Players (local multiplayer)
- vs Computer (single player)
- Move Advisor — turn on to get feedback on each move (off by default)
- No Backward Moves toggle
- Undo button
- Score tracking and move history log
