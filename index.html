Crossing The Grid

Move all your pieces to the opposite side of the board. First player to clear all 5 pieces wins.

Game concept by Sam  ·  MTST195 Group Project
Sam  ·  Mia  ·  Omar  ·  Jovin

Goal: Move all 5 of your pieces to the opposite side.

P1 (blue X) starts on rows 1–2, moves toward row 5.
P2 (orange O) starts on rows 4–5, moves toward row 1.

Legal moves:
• Move 1 step horizontally or vertically (no piece blocking).
• Jump over your own piece horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — if the square on the other side is empty.
• You cannot jump over or land on your opponent's piece.
• No backward moves (variant rule — can be toggled off).

Scoring: A piece is removed the moment it reaches the opposite side row.
